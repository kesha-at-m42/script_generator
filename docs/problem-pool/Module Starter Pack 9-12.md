**For Misconception \#8 (Comparison Errors):** \[Misconception\_\#8\]: "When numerators match, smaller denominator means BIGGER pieces\! Think about pizza \- 3/4 means 3 slices of a pizza cut into 4 pieces. 3/8 means 3 slices of a pizza cut into 8 pieces. Which gives you more pizza?"
