|---------------|----|-----------------|---------|--------------------|

| Scale Means Addition | 2 | ReadPicGraph, CreatePicGraph | SECONDARY | Student adds 5 instead of multiplying: "4 symbols = 9" |

### Open Questions / Flags

1. **CreateBarGraph EC gap:** The M3 EC tests picture graph creation, NOT bar graph creation. Bar graphs are introduced mid-late lesson. This means CreateBarGraph templates should weight toward confidence/support/baseline (building familiarity) rather than stretch/challenge (bar graph mastery is M4-M6).

2. **Comparison across representations:** The Toy Flow explicitly asks students to compare scale 2 vs scale 5 ("Which needs fewer symbols?"). This is a CONNECT operation, not assessable via standard template types. Consider a stretch/challenge comparison template that shows the same data at two scales.

3. **Skip-counting dependency:** M3 heavily relies on Grade 2 skip-counting by 5s. Students who struggle with skip-counting will struggle with both reading and creating. Remediation for #1 (Counting Symbols) should reference skip-counting as the intervention path.
