## **MODULE 7 PATH B (Singapore)**

### **LEARNING GOAL (VERBATIM):**

"Represent whole numbers on number lines given a unit fraction; write whole numbers as fractions. Locate 1 given the location of a non-unit fraction; infer the unit size and denominator."

### **CORE CONCEPTS:**

* Fractions can equal whole numbers when numerator equals denominator (b/b \= 1\)
* Any whole number can be written as a fraction
* Reverse engineering from a given fraction to find 1

### **STANDARDS:**

**Building On:** 3.NF.A.2 (fractions on number line)

**Addressing:** 3.NF.A.3.c (whole numbers as fractions)

**Building Toward:** 3.NF.A.3.d (fractions and whole number comparison)

### **VOCABULARY STAGING SEQUENCE:**

"Formalize 'whole number as fraction' (b/b \= 1\) after visual discovery" "Reintroduce flexible 'whole' (1, 2, etc.) and tie to denominator counts"

### **MISCONCEPTIONS TO ADDRESS:**

**\#2 \- Misidentifying the Whole:** Students may not recognize when a fraction equals exactly 1 **\#4 \- Unequal Spacing:** Students may place fractions without maintaining equal intervals on the number line
