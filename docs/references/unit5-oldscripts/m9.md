## **MODULE 9 PATH B (Singapore)**

### **LEARNING GOAL (VERBATIM):**

"Identify and generate equivalent fractions as the same point on a number line; Express whole numbers as fractions and recognize fractions equivalent to whole numbers."

### **CORE CONCEPTS:**

Position-based equivalence, whole number fractions

### **STANDARDS:**

**Building On:** 3.NF.A.1, 3.NF.A.2
 **Addressing:** 3.NF.A.3c
 **Building Toward:** 3.NF.A.3d

### **VOCABULARY STAGING SEQUENCE:**

Formalize "equivalent" through position; introduce whole number fractions

### **MISCONCEPTIONS TO ADDRESS:**

**\#2 \- Position confusion:** Student doesn't understand same position \= equivalent
 **\#5 \- Whole number fractions:** Student doesn't recognize fractions that equal whole numbers
 **\#7 \- Counting jumps:** Student miscounts when fractions extend beyond 1
