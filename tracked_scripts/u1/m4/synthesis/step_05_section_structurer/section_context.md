## s4_1_metacognitive_reflection_strategy_identification
# Section Summary: s4_1_metacognitive_reflection_strategy_identification

**VISUAL STATE:** A vertical bar graph in reading mode displays a single bar labeled "Data" with value 45. The graph uses a scale of 10 with axis range 0–50. Ghost gridlines are visible at 5-unit intervals (halfway marks between labeled gridlines at 10s). The Data bar is highlighted throughout the section.

**CONTENT:** This section introduced and reinforced a metacognitive strategy for reading bar graph values between gridlines: recognizing that halfway between any two lines on a scale-of-10 graph is always 5 more than the lower line (since half of 10 = 5). The student reflected on how this pattern-finding strategy enables reading any scale-of-10 bar graph, including values at and between labeled gridlines.

**STUDENT ACTION:** The student answered a multiple-choice prompt identifying which strategy helped them determine the value 45. They selected the correct option: "I found halfway—that's always 5 more," demonstrating explicit recognition of the halfway/half-of-scale pattern.
