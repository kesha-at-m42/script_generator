**CONTENT:**
This section introduced personalized recognition by recalling prior learning about matching expressions to equal groups representations. Students practiced identifying that 4 groups of 2 items corresponds to the multiplicative expression 4 × 2, reinforcing the connection between visual equal groups and symbolic multiplication notation.

**STUDENT ACTION:**
The student selected "4 × 2" from a multiple choice panel offering four options (4 × 2, 2 × 4, 4 + 2, 2 + 4) to match the displayed equal groups. The correct selection triggered affirmation dialogue, after which the multiple choice interface was hidden in preparation for the next phase where students will build expressions independently rather than select from options.
