**CONTENT:** This section introduced the concept that equations can be written in different forms—specifically, that the equal sign represents balance and equations remain true when reversed (10 = 5 × 2 is equivalent to 5 × 2 = 10). **STUDENT ACTION:** The student answered a multiple-choice judgment task evaluating whether 10 = 5 × 2 is true or false, selecting from four options: "both sides are 10," "it's just written differently," "the answer should come last," or "I'm not sure."
